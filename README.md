# PLOUF - Hydration App 

**PLOUF** is a cute and simple mobile application designed to help users track their daily water intake. Stay hydrated and healthy with a friendly interface!

## Features

*   **Daily Goal Tracking:** See your progress with a clear percentage and visual bottle.
*   **Easy Logging:** Log drinks by size (Small, Medium, Large, or Custom).
*   **Drink History:** View your stats and progress over the week with beautiful charts.
*   **Smart Reminders:** Set notifications (Morning, Noon, Evening) to never forget to drink water.
*   **Personalized Profile:** Calculate your water needs based on your weight and height.
*   **Cute UI:** A soft, blue-themed design with a friendly mascot.

## How to Use

1.  **Set Your Goal:** Enter your height and weight in the profile settings to get a suggestion.
2.  **Log a Drink:** Every time you drink water, tap the "Log Drink" button.
3.  **Check Progress:** Go to the "Your Drink Progress" tab to see your weekly stats.
4.  **Stay Notified:** Enable reminders to get a "plouf" notification on your phone!
